Why Make St. George Your Basecamp?

St. George sits at the heart of Southern Utah's red rock country. With year-round warm weather, over 300 days of sunshine, and unbeatable proximity to the region's top attractions, it's the natural choice for your Utah adventure.

Unlike staying inside the national parks, St. George offers full-service lodging, diverse dining, shopping, and entertainment — all while keeping you within striking distance of Zion, Bryce Canyon, and the Grand Canyon.

  • 45 minutes from Zion National Park
  • 2 hours from Bryce Canyon & Grand Canyon North Rim
  • 20 minutes from Snow Canyon, Sand Hollow & Quail Creek
  • Year-round golf, hiking, and outdoor recreation
  • Home to the Huntsman World Senior Games, Ironman & St. George Marathon

Major Events & Festivals

St. George hosts world-class events that draw participants and spectators from across the globe.

October

Huntsman World Senior Games

The world's largest annual multi-sport event for athletes 50+. Over 30 sports, thousands of participants, and a celebration of active aging that fills the entire city.

October

St. George Marathon

A Boston qualifier consistently ranked among America's best marathons. Point-to-point course from Pine Valley to downtown — fast, scenic, and unforgettable.

Spring / Fall

IRONMAN 70.3

The North American Championship brings elite triathletes to St. George's challenging course. The city has also hosted the IRONMAN 70.3 World Championship.
